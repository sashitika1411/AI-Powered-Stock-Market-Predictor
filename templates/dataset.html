Dataset Used in the Project

The AI-Powered Stock Market Predictor uses historical stock market data to train the machine learning model and generate predictions.

Dataset Source

The dataset is collected from publicly available stock market sources such as Yahoo Finance and stored in CSV format.

Dataset Attributes

  • Date – Trading date
  • Open – Opening price of the stock
  • High – Highest price of the day
  • Low – Lowest price of the day
  • Close – Closing price of the stock
  • Volume – Number of shares traded
